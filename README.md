# IQwiz –  Quiz App for Developers

## Description

IQwiz is a fun and interactive swift quiz app designed for entry to junior levels. Test your skills, learn from explanations, and track your progress across many questions.

Perfect for learning, practicing or preparing for interviews.
Free to use — no ads or in-app purchases.

## Features
1. Level-based progression: Junior → Mid → Senior (unlock levels as you complete them)

2. Instant feedback for correct and wrong answers.

3. Info tips for each question to help you more.

4. Track your score and high scores.

5. Display mode—system, light and dark

6.  Mute audio 

7. Quit anytime.

8. Simple and clean interface.

Open IQwiz.xcodeproj or IQwiz.xcworkspace in Xcode

Build and run on your simulator or device
